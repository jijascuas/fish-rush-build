Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

At Fish Rush, we take your privacy very seriously. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal and necessary information to provide you with a full and competitive gaming experience:

  • Username: The name you choose to enter to appear on the leaderboard (Ranking).
  • Scores: We record your game records associated with your username.
  • Anonymous Identifiers: We use Firebase Authentication anonymously. This generates a unique ID that is not linked to your real name, email, or phone number, but allows us to keep your record associated with your device.

2. Use of Information

Collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Display your position and record in the global leaderboard ("TOP 100").
  • Allow you to unlock new appearances (Extras) based on your high score.
  • Anonymously analyze technical errors to improve game performance.

3. Data Storage

We use Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) to securely store scores and rankings. Technical data is processed in accordance with Google's security policies.

In addition, the game uses your browser's local storage (localStorage) to save:

  • Your personal record (High Score).
  • Your volume preferences.
  • The selected fish appearance.

4. Third-Party Providers

Our game may use third-party services that collect information necessary for its operation:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase

5. Children's Privacy

Fish Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Your Rights

You can request the removal of your name and score from the public ranking at any time by sending us an email with your request.
